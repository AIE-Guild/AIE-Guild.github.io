About Us

Alea Iacta Est (AIE) is a online gaming community for players of massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs). Our community is built upon the belief that respect and courtesy for fellow gamers is fundamental to a worthwhile gaming experience.

This site is the hub of all software development within the AIE community.
